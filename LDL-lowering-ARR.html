Each bubble is a major lipid-lowering RCT. The x-axis is the baseline LDL of enrolled patients. The y-axis is the absolute reduction in mortality — how many deaths were actually prevented per 1,000 patients treated. Bubble size reflects trial enrollment.

ARR = (control arm mortality rate − treatment arm mortality rate) × 1,000, from published event counts and follow-up. Mortality endpoint varies by trial — CHD death (4S, WOSCOPS), vascular/CV death (CARE, LIPID, HPS, FOURIER), or all-cause (ODYSSEY Outcomes, VESALIUS). Bubble size ∝ enrollment. Dashed regression line is OLS. Vertical dashed line = VESALIUS median baseline LDL (122 mg/dL ★). VESALIUS mortality is exploratory — hierarchy broke at CHD death alone (p=0.39).
